Welcome to Ai Boxing ("App"), provided by Hamza Canbaz ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our boxing training, AI coaching, session analysis, and related premium features.

1. Introduction

By using Ai Boxing, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. The App may allow you to complete onboarding questionnaires, use camera-based live boxing sessions, receive AI-assisted coaching feedback, generate personalized training plans, track progress over time, enable notifications, and purchase premium subscriptions.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to provide and improve the App:

  • Account and Identifier Information: Anonymous authentication identifiers, internal user IDs, and purchase-related identifiers used to operate the App, associate progress with your account, and manage premium access.
  • Profile and Onboarding Information: Information you choose to provide in onboarding or settings, such as display name, experience level, fighter type, stance, training goals, training frequency, session duration preferences, training location, equipment, injuries, motivation, AI coach preferences, age, height, weight, weight goals, daily activity, meal preferences, and related profile inputs.
  • Session and Performance Data: Session timestamps, mode selections, duration, scores, score changes, punch and movement metrics, readiness indicators, focus areas, positives, biggest issues, recommended drills, streaks, weekly summaries, training plan summaries, and other boxing session results generated while you use the App.
  • Camera and Optional Media Content: Live camera frames and, if you choose to provide them, optional training footage, images, or other media used for real-time analysis, setup checks, or related product features.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including first open, onboarding completion, paywall views, purchase events, feature usage, and similar analytics events.
  • Device and Technical Information: Device, app, diagnostic, and network-related information that may be collected by Apple, Firebase, RevenueCat, or other infrastructure providers to support App functionality, diagnostics, fraud prevention, and service reliability.
  • Notification Preferences: Information about whether you enable notifications and related reminder or engagement preferences managed through your device and app settings.
  • Purchase and Subscription Information: Information related to subscriptions, one-time purchases, entitlement status, free trial status, and transaction events processed through Apple App Store and RevenueCat. We do not store your full payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your App account, session history, purchase state, and premium access.
  • To deliver live boxing sessions, readiness checks, movement analysis, coaching cues, and related in-app feedback.
  • To generate and update personalized training plans, streaks, progress summaries, and related coaching insights.
  • To save and display your profile, session history, and progress information inside the App.
  • To process purchases, restore transactions, and manage subscription or entitlement status.
  • To send reminders or notifications if you choose to enable them.
  • To monitor performance, diagnose failures, prevent abuse, and improve the App experience.
  • To respond to support requests or legal obligations where applicable.

4. Camera, Motion Analysis, and Notifications

Because the App includes camera-based training and coaching features, certain device permissions and technical processing may be used as follows:

  • Camera Access: The App requests camera access so you can use live session and movement analysis features.
  • On-Device Analysis: Live session camera input may be analyzed locally on your device to detect body position, readiness, punch mechanics, and related motion signals in real time.
  • Derived Session Data: Even when live analysis occurs on device, resulting session summaries, scores, readiness states, and related derived metrics may be saved locally and synced to our backend so your history and training plan can work across sessions.
  • Optional Media Features: If you choose to provide optional footage, images, or related media for product features, that content may be processed and stored as needed to provide the feature you requested.
  • Notifications: If you enable notifications, the App may use notification permission to send reminders, progress updates, or coaching-related engagement messages.
  • No Sale of Camera or Media Content: We do not sell your live camera input, uploaded footage, or related media content to advertisers or data brokers.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with the following categories of recipients:

  • Service Providers: Providers that help us operate the App, such as Google Firebase (authentication, cloud database, analytics, and storage where applicable), RevenueCat (subscription and purchase management), and Apple App Store (payments and in-app purchases).
  • Legal Requirements: If required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request.
  • Business Protection: When reasonably necessary to protect our rights, users, systems, or services, including investigating fraud, abuse, payment issues, or security incidents.

Depending on where you use the App, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

  • Account Records: Anonymous account identifiers, premium status, and related account records may be retained while your account remains active in our systems.
  • Profile and History Records: Onboarding profile data, session history, streak data, weekly summaries, and training plan data may remain stored until removed from our systems or no longer needed for service operation.
  • Local Device Storage: Certain profile, training plan, and session history data may also be cached on your device to improve performance and continuity.
  • Optional Media Content: Submitted footage, images, or other optional media may be retained for the time needed to support requested features, troubleshoot issues, and maintain service reliability, subject to our backend and storage workflows.
  • Analytics and Transaction Records: Analytics events and purchase-related records may be retained as needed for product improvement, accounting, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

7. Your Choices

You may have the following choices regarding your information:

  • Permissions: You can manage camera and notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Profile Information: You may update certain profile preferences within the App.
  • Subscriptions: You can manage or cancel App Store subscriptions through your Apple account settings.
  • Deletion Requests: If you want to request deletion of data associated with your App use, contact us at the email below and include enough information for us to identify your account records where possible.

8. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information, including relying on established cloud infrastructure providers for authentication, storage, database, analytics, and processing functions. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can review and delete the data where appropriate.
